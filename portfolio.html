Projects

Here are some of my recent projects:

  • Mobile Controlled (Bluetooth) Bot : I made a bot which can be controlled using our mobile via Bluetooth connection.
  • Portable Fire Alarm System for Small Industries: Developed a wireless fire safety system using NRF transceivers. Multiple sensor-equipped transmitters detect fire or smoke and instantly send signals to a central receiver. The receiver triggers an alarm and displays the specific location on an LCD screen, ensuring quick identification and response in emergencies. Ideal for flexible placement in factories and warehouses.
